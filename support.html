Having Technical Issues?

Issue with the website?

Please let me know! You can find my contact information here and please tell me anything that gave you trouble.

Having trouble contacting me?

If you aren't able to reach me, there are a couple potential problems that could be occuring.

  • I haven't responded yet

    I can sometimes be busy, or maybe already in the process of creating another art project for somebody else and just haven't had the time to get back to you! If this is the case, I promise to respond as soon as possible!

    Email address doesn't work or text/calls aren't going through

    If the email address doesn't work, please try contacting my husband at parllaxor@gmail.com. He is also in charge of running the website functionality, so you can contact him for any such issues as well!

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get something custom made?

Just feel free to contact me! You can visit the shopping page directly, or send me a text/email with a request!

How much do you charge for custom requests?

It depends on the difficulty of the art piece and how long it will take me, so when you have a request, I will charge a very reasonable price for what you've asked for!

What kinds of products do you offer?

I create normal paintings, drawings, and other types of art. Without further request you will receive the art piece, however for a small extra charge I can offer mugs, shirts, and many other options!
